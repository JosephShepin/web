Managing your QuadPrint account

Prerequisites

  1. Be connected to QuadNet (either via Ethernet or via the quadnet2.4/quadnet5 WiFi networks).
  2. Have obtained user credentials for the printing service (contact Dev to obtain credentials).

Changing your password

  1. Navigate to "https://10.8.0.5:9090/users#/USER", replacing USER with your username. If presented with a security warning, ignore the warning.
  2. Enter your QuadPrint username and password.
  3. In the portal shown, click on "Set password" to change your password.
